The Round House Restaurant has been in business since 1973, serving traditional American food. Enjoy the Best Cheeseburger in New England while enjoying the view of our beautiful gardens. Be sure to check out the signed pictures of the celebrities who have eaten here and enjoy listening to classic Rock N' Roll on the Juke Box.
